Our Story

Our story began in 2001 as young kids in elementary school, playing in the schoolyard, playing tag, and creating childhood moments that helped shape who we are today. Through our experiences in life and finding our own paths, we eventually found ourselves reconnecting. One day we decided the time was right and took that leap of faith toward happiness, and it felt like the right one.

We created memories, built connections, went on adventures, and have truly fallen in love with who we are together. By accepting our past, loving our present, and encouraging our future, we continue striving to become the people we know we can become as individuals and as a team.

Realizing this has brought us to this moment of unity. Every moment in our past and every decision we made in our present has led us to this one moment—creating a future of our happily ever after, and we wouldn’t have it any other way.

And now, we’re so excited to celebrate the next chapter of our story with all of you.
